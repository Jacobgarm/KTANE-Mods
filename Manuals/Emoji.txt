1. Determine group
Do calculations (perhaps 1 or 2) to determine which of the 5 groups of order 8 is in use.
The aarrows select different emojis, and the button submits. After a short time the result appears.
Use the Cayley Tables in appendix to find the correct group. Ismorphic emojis can also be found in appendix.
2. Determine calculation targets
The amount of options for the left and right input give row numbers which must be achieved. In easy mode, repeats still count.
3. Calculate
Find two pairs of emojis that create the 2 calculation targets. Input these in order to disarm the module.
